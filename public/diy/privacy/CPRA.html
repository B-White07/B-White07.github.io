CPRA Data Processing Statement

Welcome to our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. When using this website, we may collect and process some of your personal information to provide better services. This statement aims to inform you about how we handle and safeguard this information.

Our website utilizes Google AdSense, a service provided by Google for advertising. By using this website, you consent to us collecting, storing, and processing the following data:

  1. Cookie Information: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience on the website and to present ads in a more personalized manner. Cookies are small text files that store some information on your device.
  2. IP Address: We may log your IP address for statistical analysis and to improve website performance.
  3. Device and Browser Information: We may collect information about the device and browser you are using to ensure our website is compatible with all users.
  4. Geographical Location Data: We may use your IP address to obtain approximate geographical location information to enhance the accuracy of ad targeting.

According to CPRA regulations, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt-out of the sale of your personal information.

Please note that we do not directly process your personal data; it is processed through Google AdSense. We have entered into a data processing agreement with Google to ensure their compliance with CPRA regulations and to protect your privacy.

If you have any concerns or questions about how we process your data, feel free to contact our privacy team. Thank you for your trust and understanding.
